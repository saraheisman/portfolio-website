Hi there, I'm Sarah!

I’m excited about designing for people and solving problems that improve their lives. I draw inspiration from meeting others and hearing their stories. I love learning and for me the journey has always been the most exciting part.

My experience with the design process began as a Montessori teacher. Our classrooms emphasized the importance of having empathy for others, brainstorming solutions, then implementing, and iterating on those ideas. My interest in the design process led me to take the UX design course at Career Foundry. This experience refined my skills and encouraged my ambition to work as a UX designer.

I’ve always been passionate about immersing myself in other cultures. My time living abroad has given me the opportunity to see how people’s behaviors change across cultures. Combined with my experiences as an educator, these observations make me uniquely capable of creating UX designs that are responsive to user needs, culturally-relevant, and functional.

Skills and Knowledge

UX/UI skills include user research, user interviews, sketching, both low- and high-fidelity wireframes mockups and prototypes, usability testing, interaction and visual design. Tools include Sketch, Adobe XD, Princilple, Invision, and Adobe Photoshop. I also have experience in cross-cultural communication and research, as well as basic HTML and CSS.
